What is CSS?

CSS (Cascading Style Sheets) is used to style and design web pages. It allows you to change colors, fonts, spacing, and layout of your HTML content.

Why Learn CSS?

Learning CSS helps make your websites more attractive and user-friendly. It gives you control over how your content looks on different devices.
